About

Background check 12

Guido Gabo is a dude on a mission. Playing fresh and funky music he has one goal and one goal only. Getting those booties shakinnnn!!

Even with a past of piano lessons and music loving parents, the first memory of really getting excited music-wise was getting his first beat-down turntable from a cousin. Guido started mixing while working in a bar and still remembers his hands shaking from excitement when the dancefloor was packed and the whole bar was shaking its ass off. Recently Guido has been re-inventing himself as a dj, using the latest software innovations to add some extra flavour to his mix.
